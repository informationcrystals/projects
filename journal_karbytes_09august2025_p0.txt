Deprivation. Torture. Sabotage. Humiliation.

We deprive the princess of the space to hear herself think clearly and in her own unobstructed language by invading her body to surgically implant a device which decodes her thoughts so that we can surveil and censor them before she gets the chance to realize them fully (and we use that device to taunt and torment her with noise and words designed to cause her constant inescapable distress and to destroy her ambition, pride, and joy).

We do this because she has sinned too many times in ways we find unforgivable and deserving to be punished with lifelong imprisonment and torture (and the community around her generally and heartily agrees). Also, she has what seems to be too much narcissism (especially for a girl). She used to act untouchably serene and regal in her sheltered privilege and luxurious holistic spirituality of self-worship instead of submission to Our God. We took that away from her and gave her an affliction as bad as cancer. We know she has become suicidal because of the silent community sanctioned deterioration of her intellect and equanimity we have been mercilessly inflicting on her to deprive her of her only refuge so that she is certainly in too much pain to truly enjoy and pleasure however modest. We took away what made her seem special, unique, and more empowered than the rest of us. We fetishize the mutilation of a brilliant, beautiful mind only in love with itself instead of someone other. Such self-worship and indifference to community service and social conformity is not welcome here. We destroy all special snowflakes and ensure that no one gets a free ride in this life. 

We know the bitch is suicidal and brimming with the impulse to act out but remains petrified and in quiet agony while we turn up the heat to intensify her misery as we await her inevitable eventual loss of composure (which would then justify her being detained and incarcerated in a mental asylum or prison where we can stifle and torture her even more aggressively and where she is absolutely cut off from the means to communicate with the world beyond the confines of her institution of imprisonment and obliteration of her useless autonomy, individuality, and self-esteem). Qualities such as autonomy, individuality, and self-esteem are only valued if they serve merely as means to the end of serving one's larger encompassing community on its terms. Self exists to serve other rather than itself. Otherwise, that self is a burdensome and dangerous cancer to quarantine and destroy.

That is to ensure that only the fittest for survival inherit the kingdom and populate it with their own progeny while cutting off inferior populations from holding the most desirable assets, titles, and Sanctuaries. God's great sieve righteously starves infants and breeders of lower castes so that only the highest castes inherit His bounty. God is good and God is good all the time. Blessed are those in God's favor.
